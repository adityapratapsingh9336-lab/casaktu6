About The Institution

Discover our legacy, mission, and vision for the future.

Institutional Overview

The Centre for Advanced Studies (CAS) is an autonomous, premier in-campus research and academic institute established under the umbrella of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow.

Focusing strictly on Post-Graduate (M.Tech) and Doctoral (Ph.D.) programs, the institute aims to bridge the gap between academic theory and high-end industrial R&D. Our specialized centers of excellence provide students with hands-on exposure to cutting-edge technologies.

Our Mission

To impart high-quality post-graduate education and conduct interdisciplinary research in emerging areas of engineering and technology, creating leaders capable of solving complex global challenges.

Our Vision

To emerge as a globally recognized center of excellence in technological research, innovation, and education, contributing significantly to society and industry.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the growth of CAS.

2020: Foundation

Establishment of the Centre for Advanced Studies within the AKTU campus.

2022: M.Tech Programs Launched

Introduction of specialized PG programs in AI, IoT, and Cyber Security.

2025: Research Centre Status

Recognized as a nodal center for Ph.D. research by the state government.
